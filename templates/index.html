Who We Are

Angle of Health Trust

A registered Zimbabwean trust dedicated to community development through health services and practical skills education. NtandoMods is our flagship training programme - empowering youth and adults with employable skills.

🏥

Health-Focused Mission

Our courses are delivered under the health and wellness umbrella, equipping communities with skills that improve livelihoods and wellbeing.

🎓

Certified Training

Every graduate receives an official NtandoMods certificate recognised by employers across Zimbabwe. Quality training, quality results.

🇿🇼

Made for Zimbabwe

All materials are written for the Zimbabwean context - EcoCash, ZIMRA, ZiG currency, local business practices. Relevant and practical.

💪

No Barriers to Entry

Zero O-Levels required. Affordable fees with payment plans. We believe everyone deserves access to quality education and employment skills.
